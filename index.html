Akan names come from Ghanaian culture, where children are named based on the day they were born.This application calculates the day of the week a user was born and assigns them an Akan name based on their gender. Here is a simple table to help you understand how akan names are assigned.

DayMale NameFemale Name
Sunday Kwasi Akosua
Monday Kwadwo Adwoa
Tuesday Kwabena Abenaa
Wednesday Kwaku Akua
Thursday Yaw Yaa
Friday Kofi Afua
Sarturday Kwame Ama

This is are the Akan names and their coresponding days of the week.
